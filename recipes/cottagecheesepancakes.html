Cottage Cheese Pancakes

Description

High protein breakfast food that is nutritious and delicious! Recipe serves four.

Ingredients

  • 0.75 cups cottage cheese
  • 0.25 cups plain greek yogurt
  • 2 eggs
  • 0.5 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 0.5 tbsp baking powder
  • 0.5 cup flour
  • 0.13 cup canola oil for frying

Steps

  1. optional: For a more smooth pancake texture, blend cottage cheese in a blender before. Flour can be substituted for double the amount of grounded oats as homemade oat flour.
  2. Combine all ingredients in the following order - cheese, eggs, yogurt, vanilla extract, sugar, baking powder and flour. Mix in each time you add an ingredient.
  3. Preheat a skillet with canola oil on a medium heat. Fry the cottage cheese pancakes on each side until golden brown.
  4. Serve with maple syrup, jam, fruit, etc. Best while they are still hot and the cheese inside is melted.
